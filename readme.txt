       Introduction
This document serves as a guide for users of the Magnifico Login Seguro do Manuel, detailing how to create an account,
log in, and navigate the application. This application provides secure access to registered users.

    Getting Started
Account Creation
To use the application, you first need to create an account:

Navigate to the Create Account Page: From the homepage, click on the "Create Account" link.
    Fill Out the Form:
Username: Choose a unique username.
Password Requirements: Your password must be at least 8 characters long, include at least two uppercase letters, one
special character (!@#$&*), two digits, and three lowercase letters.
Confirm Password: Re-enter your password to confirm it.
Submit: Click the "Create Account" button to submit your information. If your username is unique and your password meets
 the complexity requirements, your account will be created. Otherwise, you will receive an error message.
Example of a valid password: Aa!12bcd

    Logging In
After creating an account, you can log in to the application:

Navigate to the Login Page: From the homepage, click on the "Login" link.
Enter Your Credentials:
Username: Enter the username you registered with.
Password: Enter your password.
Submit: Click the "Login" button to access the application. If your credentials are correct, you will be redirected to
a page indicating you're logged in. If not, an error message will appear.

    Viewing the Logged In Page
Once logged in, you will be taken to the "You're Logged In" page, where you'll see a welcome message along with your
username. This page serves as the landing area for additional content and features available to registered users.

    Logging Out
To log out of the application:

Navigate to the Logout Option: On the "You're Logged In" page, locate and click the "Logout" link or button.
Confirmation: You will be redirected to the homepage, and your session will be ended.

    Troubleshooting
Forgot Password: Currently, if you forget your password, you will need to create a new account.
Account Creation Issues: Ensure your chosen username is unique and your password meets the specified complexity
requirements.
Login Issues: Verify that your username and password are entered correctly. Remember that passwords are case-sensitive.
